Room repartition algorithm

   This was done by creating all possible permutations of adults, search of available rooms based on their capacity specifications and further addition of childrens based on age groups. Repartitions had a score depending on the number of rooms and the remaining empty occupancies in them. The repartition with the highest score is the one having the smallest number of rooms in which you can fit exactly the highest number of travellers.
